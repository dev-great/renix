How to Use
How to Use the Renix Academy Platform for an Enriched Learning Experience
  1. Create an account to get started.
  2. Choose a learning package that best suits your needs.
  3. Subscribe to your selected package.
  4. Log in to gain full access after subscribing.
  5. Explore the dashboard and familiarize yourself with its features.
  6. Access the question bank and create practice tests in either Study Mode or Exam Mode.
  7. Take the readiness assessment to evaluate your exam preparedness.
  8. Review your performance after each test to track progress and identify areas for improvement.
What is Renix Academy International?
Renix Academy International is the premier learning environment for aspiring Nigerian nurses and midwives. It was developed by practicing nurses and seasoned educators in nursing and midwifery to provide a smarter, more effective way of learning.
Who Founded the Academy?
Renix Academy was founded by Merchant Nurse, a well-known figure in the Nigerian nursing community.
FAQs
How long is my subscription?
The Academy currently offers a monthly subscription, valid for 30 days.
How do I Log in to the Platform?
To log in, simply use the email address and password you registered with. The Academy recommends using a Gmail account when creating your account for the best experience.
