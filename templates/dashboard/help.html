How to Use
How to use Renix Academy platform for enrich learning experiences
  1. Start by creating an account
  2. Choose the package of learning that suits you
  3. Subscribe to that package
  4. Login to gain full access after your subscription
  5. Go to the dashboard and familiarize yourself with it
  6. Move to the question bank and create practice questions in either study mode or exam mode
  7. Take the readiness assessment to evaluate your exam preparedness
  8. Review your performance after each test you create or complete
