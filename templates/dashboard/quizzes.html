READINESS ASSESSMENT

  • This is the idea sample of questions you are likely to be confronted with during your Post UTME/Nursing School
    entrance examination, covering all areas of this practice app and they are structured to test how prepared you are to take your exam.
  • Total questions in each assessment are 250 and it will last 180 minutes.
  • Once you start you cannot pause the assessment
  • You will need to have your own calculator to work on the calculations.
  • At the end of the assessment your results will be reviewed and ranked according to how you perform compared to
    other candidate who have taken this assessment
  • The algorithm will analysis your results and give you the probability of you passing this exam with your level of
    preparedness
  • You can decide to end the practice halfway and the system will rank your performance with respect to total questions
